What It Is

What is the Unleashed Operators Circle?

The Unleashed Operators Circle™ is a private, application-only mastermind for founders, builders, and operators who are deploying and scaling real AI systems. It is curated by Fer Patel and limited to a small circle of serious operators.

Not a community.
A circle.

The Unleashed Operators Circle™ Mastermind is a private, curated group of serious operators — assembled personally by Fer Patel — who come together bi-weekly to solve real problems, share real wins, and build the kind of relationships that actually move businesses forward.

This is not a course. There's no passive consumption here. Every member is expected to show up, contribute, and execute. The room only works when the right people are in it.

Membership is not purchased. It is applied for, reviewed, and granted based on fit, commitment, and the value you bring to the circle.

"You're not paying for content. You're investing in the room — and in who you become by being in it."
